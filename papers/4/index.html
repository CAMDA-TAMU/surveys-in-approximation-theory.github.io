Surveys in Approximation Theory, 2 (2006), 1-52.

Constructive Function Theory on Sets of the Complex Plane through Potential Theory and Geometric Function Theory

V. V. Andrievskii

Abstract. This is a survey of some recent results concerning polynomial inequalities and polynomial approximation of functions in the complex plane. The results are achieved by the application of methods and techniques of modern geometric function theory and potential theory.
